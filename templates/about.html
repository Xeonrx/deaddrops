Deaddrops was developed by Xeonrx, and goes by the MIT license. You can find the source at: https://github.com/Xeonrx/deaddrops

With a given subnet value, Deaddrops will discover active & reachable hosts. The Nmap module is then used to detect the system that runs under reachable IP addresses.


Deaddrops uses Flask to peform via webapp, and is only a mere project. Issues/bugs can be filled out at: https://github.com/Xeonrx/deaddrops/issues
